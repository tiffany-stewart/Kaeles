YOURSHELF

OVERVIEW

YourShelf is a streamlined audiobook app that allows users to enjoy their personal library, and experience their literary journey. We have been able to prioritize feasibility, without sacrificing organization. With the interface design allowing users to both listen and read their favorite authors, we have placed emphasis on building connections between users and their go-to publications.
